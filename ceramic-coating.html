Ceramic Coating

Permanent paint protection with mirror-like gloss. The ultimate shield against Delhi's harsh elements.

The Science

Nano-Ceramic Technology

Ceramic coating is a liquid polymer that chemically bonds with your car's factory paint, creating a permanent layer of protection. Unlike waxes or sealants that wash away, ceramic coatings form a semi-permanent bond that lasts for years.

At Velorix, we apply multi-layer ceramic and graphene coatings that deliver extreme hydrophobic properties, 9H pencil hardness, UV resistance, and a depth of gloss that makes your paint look perpetually wet.

Our Method

Application Process

A meticulous multi-step process that ensures a flawless, long-lasting result.

01

Deep Wash

Multi-stage wash with iron fallout removal, clay bar decontamination, and chemical cleaning.

02

Paint Correction

Machine polishing to remove swirls, scratches, and imperfections. Creates a mirror-flat surface.

03

Panel Wipe

IPA wipe-down to remove all polishing oils and residues. Clean surface for maximum bonding.

04

Coating Application

Layer-by-layer ceramic application in controlled environment with infrared curing between coats.
