What is Ceramic Coating?

Liquid Glass For Your Vehicle

Ceramic coating is a liquid polymer that chemically bonds to your vehicle's paint, creating a permanent or semi-permanent layer of protection. Unlike wax or sealant, it doesn't wash away — it becomes part of your paint surface.

We use certified products from Gtechniq, Gyeon, and CarPro to deliver coatings that last 2-7 years depending on the package. The result? Mirror-like gloss, extreme water beading, and effortless maintenance.

Our Method

Application Process

A meticulous multi-day process ensures perfect bonding and maximum durability.

01

Deep Wash & Decontamination

Thorough wash, clay bar treatment, iron fallout removal, and tar removal to achieve a perfectly clean surface.

02

Paint Correction

Machine polishing to remove swirls, scratches, and oxidation. The coating locks in this corrected finish permanently.

03

Coating Application

Panel-by-panel application in our dust-free chamber under controlled lighting. Each layer cures before the next is applied.

04

IR Curing & Inspection

Infrared curing accelerates bonding. Final inspection under LED panels ensures 100% coverage with no high spots.
