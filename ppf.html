What is PPF?

Invisible Shield For Your Paint

Paint Protection Film is a transparent, self-healing thermoplastic urethane film applied to your vehicle's painted surfaces. It absorbs impacts from stone chips, road debris, bug splatter, bird droppings, and minor scratches — keeping your paint pristine underneath.

We use only certified XPEL Ultimate Plus and Llumar Valor films — the gold standard in PPF technology — with computer-cut patterns for precise, seamless coverage on every panel.

Why PPF?

Key Benefits

PPF is the best investment for protecting your vehicle's value and appearance on Indian roads.

◆

Stone Chip Protection

Absorbs impacts from gravel, road debris, and construction dust — essential for Indian highways and city roads.

◇

Self-Healing Surface

Light scratches and swirl marks disappear with heat exposure — the film literally heals itself in sunlight.

◈

UV & Chemical Resistance

Blocks harmful UV rays that cause paint fading, and resists bird droppings, tree sap, and acid rain damage.

▣

Preserves Resale Value

Factory paint stays in showroom condition underneath. When you sell, the paint is as good as day one.

⬡

Invisible Protection

Ultra-clear optical grade film is virtually undetectable — your car looks stock while being fully protected.

✧

Easy Maintenance

Hydrophobic top coat repels water and dirt. Regular washing is all you need — no special maintenance required.
