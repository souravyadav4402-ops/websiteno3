What is PPF?

Your Paint's Invisible Guardian

Paint Protection Film is a thermoplastic polyurethane film applied to your car's painted surfaces. It's optically clear, virtually invisible, and protects against stone chips, road debris, bug acids, bird droppings, mineral deposits, and UV damage.

Our premium self-healing PPF from XPEL and Llumar uses advanced top-coat technology that allows minor scratches and swirl marks to disappear with heat — keeping your car looking showroom-fresh for years.
