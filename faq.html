Knowledge Base

Common Questions

How long does a full body car wrap take?

+

A full body wrap typically takes 3-5 working days depending on the vehicle's complexity, the type of wrap material, and whether any disassembly is required. We don't rush — precision takes time.

Will a vinyl wrap damage my original paint?

+

No. High-quality cast vinyl wraps are completely paint-safe and fully reversible. When removed professionally, they leave your factory paint untouched and in the same condition as before installation.

How long does a car wrap last?

+

Premium 3M and Avery Dennison wraps last 5-7 years with proper care. Factors like sun exposure, washing habits, and parking conditions can affect longevity. We provide detailed aftercare guidance.

What's the difference between PPF and ceramic coating?

+

PPF is a thick physical film that protects against stone chips, scratches, and physical damage. Ceramic coating is a liquid-applied chemical layer that provides hydrophobic properties, UV protection, and enhanced gloss. For maximum protection, many clients combine both — PPF underneath with ceramic on top.

Do you offer warranty on your services?

+

Yes. All our wraps come with installation warranty plus material manufacturer warranty (5-7 years for wraps, up to 10 years for PPF). Ceramic coatings include warranty certificates from the coating brand. Specific terms vary by product.

Can I wrap a leased or financed car?

+

Absolutely. Since vinyl wraps are fully reversible and don't damage the paint, they're perfect for leased vehicles. You can personalize your car during the lease period and remove the wrap before returning it.

How much does a full body wrap cost?

+

Full body wraps start from ₹75,000 for standard colors and go up to ₹1,50,000+ for exotic finishes like color-shift or brushed metal. Price depends on vehicle size, material choice, and complexity. We provide free detailed quotes.

Do I need to do anything to maintain my wrap?

+

Basic care: hand wash only (no automated brushes), avoid harsh chemicals, and park in shade when possible. We provide a complete aftercare guide with every installation. Matte wraps require matte-specific products.

Is ceramic coating worth it for daily drivers?

+

Absolutely. Delhi's pollution, hard water, bird droppings, and harsh sun make ceramic coating especially valuable here. It drastically reduces maintenance effort and keeps your paint protected from daily environmental damage.

Do you provide pick-up and delivery?

+

Yes, we offer complimentary pick-up and delivery within Rohini and nearby areas for services above ₹25,000. For other locations in Delhi NCR, nominal transport charges apply. Ask us for details.
