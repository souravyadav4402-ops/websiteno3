How long does a full body wrap take?

+

A full body wrap typically takes 4-7 working days depending on the vehicle size and complexity. SUVs like Fortuner and Range Rover take longer than sedans. We never rush quality — proper prep, installation, and post-heat treatment all take time.

Will wrapping damage my original paint?

+

No, not at all. Premium vinyl wraps from 3M and Avery Dennison are designed to be removable without damaging factory paint. In fact, wrapping protects your original paint from UV damage, minor scratches, and environmental contaminants. When removed, your paint will be in the same condition as when the wrap was applied.

How long does a vinyl wrap last?

+

With proper care, a premium vinyl wrap lasts 5-7 years. This depends on exposure to sun, how the car is washed, and whether it's garaged. In Delhi's climate, we recommend indoor parking when possible and avoiding automatic car washes with harsh brushes.

What's the difference between PPF and ceramic coating?

+

PPF (Paint Protection Film) is a thick, clear film that physically protects against stone chips, scratches, and impacts. Ceramic coating is a liquid chemical bond that provides gloss, hydrophobic properties, and UV protection but doesn't prevent physical damage. For maximum protection, we recommend PPF topped with ceramic coating.

Do you provide warranty on your work?

+

Yes, all our services come with warranty. Wraps carry 3-7 year warranties depending on the package. PPF comes with manufacturer warranty of up to 10 years. Ceramic coatings are warrantied for 2-7 years. We provide proper documentation and warranty cards for every project.

Can I wrap a leased or financed car?

+

Absolutely. Since wraps are 100% removable without damaging the paint, they're perfect for leased vehicles. You get to enjoy a custom look during your lease period, and the wrap can be removed before return with no trace left behind. Many of our clients wrap their leased BMWs and Mercedes for this exact reason.

How much does a full body wrap cost?

+

Full body wraps start from ₹75,000 for sedans using premium 3M or Avery Dennison film. SUVs start from ₹90,000. Premium finishes like color shift or chrome wraps start from ₹1,20,000. Exact pricing depends on vehicle size, film choice, and complexity. We provide free quotes after vehicle inspection.

How do I maintain my wrapped/coated car?

+

Hand wash only with pH-neutral car shampoo. Avoid automatic car washes with brushes. For ceramic coated cars, water and dirt will largely slide off on their own. We provide a detailed aftercare guide with every project and stock maintenance products at our studio.

Is wrapping/PPF safe for daily drivers?

+

Yes, absolutely. The majority of our clients are daily drivers in Delhi NCR traffic. Wraps and PPF are designed to handle everyday conditions — sun exposure, rain, road debris, and parking lot encounters. In fact, daily drivers benefit the most from the protection these films provide.

Do you offer pick-up and delivery?

+

Yes, we offer complimentary pick-up and delivery within Delhi NCR for all projects above ₹50,000. For projects below this amount, pick-up/delivery is available at a nominal charge. We can also collect new cars directly from the dealership for pre-delivery PPF or ceramic coating installation.
