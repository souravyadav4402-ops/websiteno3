Our Story

Born From Passion, Built On Precision

Velorix Customs started in 2016 as a small garage in Rohini with a big vision — to bring world-class vehicle customization to Delhi NCR. What began as two enthusiasts wrapping their own cars has grown into a full-scale premium automotive studio.

Today, we operate from a 5,000 sq ft purpose-built facility with climate-controlled bays, dust-free chambers, and a team of 15+ certified installers trained in the latest techniques from 3M, Avery Dennison, and XPEL.

From BMW owners in South Delhi to Fortuner enthusiasts in Gurgaon, we've earned the trust of 2,500+ clients who demand nothing but the best for their vehicles.
