Who We Are

The Velorix Legacy

Founded in 2016 in Rohini, Delhi, Velorix Customs was born from an obsession with automotive perfection. What started as a small vinyl wrapping workshop has evolved into a full-spectrum vehicle customization studio trusted by car enthusiasts, luxury car owners, and fleet operators across Delhi NCR.

Our team of certified installers brings over 8 years of hands-on experience with premium materials from 3M, Avery Dennison, XPEL, and Llumar. Every project is a testament to our commitment — no shortcuts, no compromises, only excellence.

We believe every vehicle tells a story. Our job is to make sure yours turns heads.
